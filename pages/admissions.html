Admissions

Welcome to Kashenyi Secondary School! We are excited to welcome new students to our vibrant and diverse community. Our admissions process is designed to be transparent, supportive, and accessible to all prospective students and their families.

Admission Requirements

  • Completed application form (available at the school or online)
  • Copy of previous academic records or report cards
  • Birth certificate or valid identification
  • Two recent passport-size photographs
  • Transfer letter (for students joining from another school)

Application Process

  1. Obtain and fill out the application form from the school office or download it from our website.
  2. Submit the completed form along with the required documents to the admissions office.
  3. Attend an interview and/or entrance assessment (if applicable).
  4. Wait for the admission decision, which will be communicated via phone or email.
  5. Upon acceptance, complete the enrollment process and pay the necessary fees.
