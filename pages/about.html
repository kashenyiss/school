About Kashenyi Secondary School

Kashenyi Secondary School is a center of academic excellence and holistic development, dedicated to nurturing responsible, innovative, and empowered students. Established with a vision to provide quality education, we strive to create a supportive environment where every learner can thrive.

Our Mission

To provide quality education that will enable both boys and girls achieve success in diversity of opportunities and become self reliant

Our Vision

Holistic education to all our children.

Our Values

  • Being honest
  • Tolerant
  • Clean
  • Accommodative
  • Hard working
  • Discipline
  • God fearing
  • Self respect
